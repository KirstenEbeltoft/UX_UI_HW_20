ABOUT THE PROBLEM RESEARCH ITERATION THE SOLUTION

Tomorrow’s Rainbow is a nonprofit organization in Coconut Creek, FL that provides instrumental support to children who are suffering from the loss of a loved one.

By combining traditional methods of healing, such as peer groups and individual therapy, with animal therapy, they are able to cultivate an environment for families to best cope with distressful life events and provide meaningful experiences.

The Problem

Although Tomorrow’s Rainbow is providing a vital service to local families, we have observed their website does not meet adequate UX/UI standards. We believe this organization could gain more traction by bringing their online presence up to standard.

Research

To better understand users’ needs and desires for Tomorrow’s Rainbow, we conducted 4 interviews and collected 57 surveys.

The Solution

We created a style guide and design that gave Tomorrow’s Rainbow a significant face lift, while keeping the spirit of the organization at the forefront of our design decision-making.

Style Guide

We based the color palette off of their original logo, which we did not modify. We incorporated the “rainbow” aesthetic in a way that still felt cohesive and complementary.

Redesign

Unfortunately, we were unable to connect with our client about redesign specifics. In light of this hiccup, we decided to use either the same written copy from the original site or a slightly modified version of it, as well as using pictures and colorways from the original site to bolster the redesign.

Reflection

    What Works
    What could be improved
  • Clear navigation and color contrast on both mobile and desktop versions
  • Adding an image on the mobile homepage to break up content
  • Added testimonial page gives more inspiration to try their services
  • Increase the font size on the desktop version for better legibility
  • Mobile text is clear and legible without being overwhelming
  • Adjusting the footer and header names to match main navigation
  • Clear call to actions, button link text, and banners
  • Adding a donation button on the mobile version for consistency with the desktop
  • Images to show and express emotion from their past events
  • Remove lines on the mobile navigation for a cleaner appearance
